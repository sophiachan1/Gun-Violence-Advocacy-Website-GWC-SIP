Types of Gun Violence:


Homicide:


Firearm homicide is using a gun with the intent to kill which can include one target or multiple targets like a mass shooting. More than 1/3rd of all gun deaths are due to homicide. But when looking at all homicide cases, about 75% of homicides were with the usage of a gun.


Suicide:


Suicide and gun accessibility are intertwined. It is the leading cause of gun related deaths as more than half of suicide deaths are with the use of a gun because of how easy it is for someone to get a gun and the effectiveness of a gun.


Crime or Assult:


A crime can be defined as an unlawful act. An assult is defined as a physical attack on someone's body. And in this case, a crime or assult with the usage of a gun. In both cases, guns are used more as a weapon to harm than it is used as protection.


Domestic Violence:


About 1 in 4 women and 1 in 7 women experience domestic violence by a partner. Guns are a contributing factor to that statistic as it is a tool used to threaten, control, or even kill. Over half of partner homicides are with a gun and a women whose partner has access to a gun is 5 times more likely to be murdered by them.


Police Brutality:


The idea of white supremacy and systemic racism creates an environement that is harmful to the black community by a force that was intended to protect people. 95% of deaths that are caused by a police is through the usage of a firearm and a black person is 3 times more likely to be shot and killed by a police officer compared to a white person.


Accidental:


Accidents happen but with how easy and how many people do have access to a gun at home or on themselves, it is so easy for an accident to happen with people of all ages and not just minors.
