Demand Change And End Gun Violence.

Gun Violence: an act committed with the use of a gun.


The goal is to spread the word about gun violence and how we can prevent and control it as soon as possible.


Gun violence is an epidemic and a complex problem that is still yet to be fixed in the US. There is an estimate of about 39,000 people who die because of guns each year. Don't wait for someone you know and love to be a part of that growing statistic and act now.
